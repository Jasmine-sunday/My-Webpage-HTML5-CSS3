Master/Slave Electronic Control System

As a technician, you are asked by your supervisor to setup an Electronic control
system for following scenario.
Scenario:
An operator in the factory is doing her job at her station. After someone knocks on
the small sliding door, she needs to walk a few meters from her station from her sliding door
so that people from outside could drop some documents on the tray through
the sliding door. She would like to have a system where she could open the little
sliding door from her station by putting her hand on the sensor to toggle the opening and
closing of the sling door. She will be notified by a LED indicator if the sling door is
open or close.
